about

Hey there! I´m Cristina, nice to meet you! In case you want to know a bit about me, I´m from Spain and I was born in 1991.

As far as I can remember, I have been drawing since I was a little girl and from a very early age I have always known that I wanted to turn my passion and my hobbie into my way of earning a living.

I always try to use part of my free time to keep updated learning new technics and, of course, to draw things I like, although I must admit that I can´t do it as much as I would like.

At first I wanted to make comics, but I have ended working as a Game Artist. And I love it!
